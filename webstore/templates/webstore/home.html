About Us

Welcome to Lush Lawns Mowing Parts!

Let us tell you little about ourselves.

It all started when a group of guys who wanted to make some money while in college took a class together. All the guys cut lawns as kids to make a little money, and we decided to open a small business that sells lawnmower parts and accessories for the most common lawnmowers in our area. We felt like something was still missing within our business. We decided to implement a website for our small business. This website will allow customers to purchase parts via the web.

We have a large stock of lawnmower parts for the most common lawnmowers within our area. Our customers can choose based on the brands they love.

We love to help people get parts for their lawnmower so, they can care for their lawn. Lawn care is often overlooked or underestimated, but maintaining a well-kept lawn offers numerous benefits. A healthy lawn not only enhances the curb appeal of your property but also provides a welcoming environment.
